Internship

Legal Adhikar understands young students' appetite for exposure and experience in the legal industry. We have curated an internship opportunity for law students which provides more than a glimpse of what goes inside a law firm.

One-to-one interactions with partners, associates and society help the volunteers direct their analytical skills towards complex legal situations by applying the principles of law.

With training and development on the go, students will be able to develop interpersonal skills and groom themselves becoming a better professional.

What we are looking for?

We are certainly looking for students who have the drive and zeal to be a part of this ever-growing law firm – Legal Adhikar. The students should be able to possess –

  • Good communication skills (should be able to express his/her views)
  • Team player quality
  • Right attitude with the temperament of learning

What do we offer?

Legal Adhikar offers students pursuing an education in law, a one /two /three/six week internship in two modes i.e- Virtual and Physical Mode.

How can you apply?

We request you to send in your application, four to eight weeks before the preferred date of the internship, enabling us to get back to you two to four weeks before the commencement of those dates. You could either upload your resume and complete the form or email us on the below-mentioned addresses as per your preferred mode:

  • Virtual internship
  • Physical internship
